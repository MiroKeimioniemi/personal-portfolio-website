This academic paper examines the pricing strategies employed by companies in the digital music production software industry.

## Abstract

The digital goods market presents unique challenges for pricing strategies, particularly in industries where piracy is prevalent. This study analyzes the music production software industry to understand how companies respond to piracy threats through their pricing decisions.
