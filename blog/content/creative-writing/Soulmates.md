---
title: Soulmates
date: 2021-11-02
type: creative
categories: ["Poem"]
---

Best Friends for life,\
Lovers by night,\
Partners in crime\
already at first sight

  
Calculating and Kind,\
one Steady,\
the other Full of Life,\
both Adventure-Inclined

  
Fire and Night,\
Dark complements the Light\
only Opposites may find\
The Golden Compromise
