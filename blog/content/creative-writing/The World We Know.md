Picture a world, more aware and connected than ever,

full of fascinating people, brilliant ideas and beauty.

Everyone has a voice, chance for discovery and access

to all kinds of ingenuity.



Inspiration, amusement, entertainment.

Global news and debate, current topics, climate crisis, banking crisis, meaning crisis, war terrorism violence storms crime poverty faminexploitationpolarizationlies

distraction, procrastination,

isolation.

  

Existential threats of asteroids, AI, overconsumption, super volcanoes, solar flames, pole shift, politicians, pollution, nuclear war are all 

certain, impossible, likely, improbable, now, tomorrow, yesterday, soon, never, everyday, here, there, somewhere, nowhere, everywhere.
